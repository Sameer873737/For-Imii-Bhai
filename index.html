Sachhai aur Maafi

Bhai, main janta hoon main galat tha. Shaista se shadi se pehle is tarah baat karna aur boundaries cross karna Islamically aur as a family bhi theek nahi tha. Aapka gussa bilkul jayaz hai.

Ek bade bhai ke naate aapne jo bhi react kiya, woh aapka haq tha apni behen ki hifazat ke liye. Main apni is galti ko bina kisi shart ke manta hoon aur Allah se bhi tauba karta hoon, aur aapse bhi haath jod kar maafi mangta hoon.

🤝

Hamar Apnapan

Bhai, kya aapko yaad hai woh waqt jab hum sirf dost the? Aapke mushkil waqt mein, maine hamesha koshish ki thi ki ek sacche dost aur bhai ki tarah aapke saath khada rahun.

Meri dosti kabhi dikhawa nahi thi. Jo izzat aur mohabbat mere dil mein aapke liye tab thi, woh aaj bhi hai. Ek galti ki wajah se kya humari woh dosti, woh loyalty poori tarah khatam ho jayegi? Please us waqt ko yaad karein jab hum ek doosre ke dukh-dard samajhte the.

💍

Pak Niyat aur Sacchayi

Meri niyat Shaista ke liye hamesha pak rahi hai. Main uski izzat karta hoon, usey chahta hoon, aur uske saath ek halal aur khubsurat zindagi basana chahta hoon.

Uske aur aap sab ke liye, main har badi qurbani dene ko tayyar hoon. Yahan tak ki apne future ke liye apni nationality change karne jaisa bada step bhi lene ko raazi hoon, taaki main ek zimmedar shohar ban sakun aur aap sab ka sar fakhr se ooncha rakh sakun.

Yeh meri aapse ek mard ki mard se zubaan hai. Main usey hamesha khush rakhunga.

Aakhiri Guzarish

Shaista ki khushi aur uski marzi ka khayal rakhte hue, kya aap is chote bhai ko ek aakhiri mauka de sakte hain? Main sab kuch sahi tarike se, aapki ijazat aur duaon ke saath karna chahta hoon. Ek bar mil lijiye, mujhe apni baat sabit karne ka mauka dijiye.
